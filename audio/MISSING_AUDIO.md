## How to Add Audio

### Option 1: Forvo.com
1. Search for word at https://forvo.com
2. Download MP3 pronunciation
3. Rename to match convention (capitalized, with umlauts)
4. Place in `audio/words_from_duolingo/`

### Option 2: Google Translate
1. Go to translate.google.com
2. Enter German word
3. Click speaker icon and record
4. Save as `Word.mp3`

### Option 3: Text-to-Speech Tools
- Use German TTS with proper pronunciation
- Ensure natural intonation
- Save with proper naming
